== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `team-builder` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Tea Builder Panels menu to customize this plugin.
4. Copy your publish Team shortcode and then paste into your page or post to display it.
